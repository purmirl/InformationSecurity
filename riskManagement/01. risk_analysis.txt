01. Quantitative Risk Analysis
 01-1. Basic Function
  . SLE = ASSET_VALUE * EF
  . ALE = SLE * ARO = ASSET_VALUE * EF * ARO

 01-2. Description
  . EF : Exposure Factor
  . SLE : Single Loss Expectancy
  . ASSET_VALUE : value of things or asset
  . ARO : Annualized Rate of Occurrence
  . ALE : Annualized Loss Expectancy
